  1. Recent work on the interplay between neutrinos and dark matter in the early Universe: Freezing-in Dark Matter from Secret Neutrino Interactions
    • Key points:

      • We invent a model with dark sector that only contains a fermionic dark matter and a light scalar mediator. The dark scalar talks with the SM only through the light scalar mediator through its interactions with SM neutrinos.
      • We find that our model is in tension with experimental constraints while trying to solve the small-scale structure problems and dark matter indirect detection at Super-K simultaneously.
    • Key plots:

      • Left plot: Benchmark points that can solve the small-scale structure problems.
      • Right plot: Parameter space that survives from dark matter indirect detection.
